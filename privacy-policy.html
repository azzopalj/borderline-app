Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

The short version

  • We collect no personal information
  • No account or sign-in required
  • All your stats and game history are stored on your device only
  • No third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising
  • In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple
  • Network requests only go to MLB's public data APIs

1. Overview

Borderline ("the app", "we", "us") is a baseball game for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

The simple truth: Borderline does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app functions entirely on your device.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • No account required. You do not create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details to use the app.
  • No analytics. We do not use any analytics SDK (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude). We collect no usage data, crash logs, or behavioral telemetry.
  • No advertising. The app contains no advertisements and no ad tracking.
  • No location data. The app never requests or accesses your location.
  • No contacts, camera, or microphone. The app does not request access to any device sensors or personal data stores.

3. Data Stored On Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Game statistics — your accuracy percentages, streaks, points, and pitch history, stored via Apple's SwiftData framework in the app's private sandbox.
  • Daily Challenge completion status — a date string stored in UserDefaults to track whether you've played today's challenge.
  • Last known game date — a date string stored in UserDefaults for the off-season fallback feature.
  • Premium unlock status — managed entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. We never see or store your payment information.

This data is stored in your app's private container. It is included in iCloud backups if you have iCloud backup enabled, but is not accessible to us.

4. Network Requests

The app makes network requests to the following public data sources to fetch baseball game data:

  • MLB Stats API (statsapi.mlb.com) — for game schedules, live pitch data, and umpire assignments. This is a public API. No personal data is included in these requests.
  • Baseball Savant / Statcast (baseballsavant.mlb.com) — for pitch coordinate data used in Arcade mode. This is a public endpoint. No personal data is included in these requests.

No user identifiers, device IDs, or personal information are included in any network request. Requests consist only of query parameters for game dates and game IDs.

5. In-App Purchases

Borderline offers a one-time optional premium unlock ("Borderline Pro"). All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We never receive, store, or process any payment card information or billing details.

Apple's privacy policy applies to all purchase transactions: apple.com/legal/privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

Borderline does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app collects no personal information from any user regardless of age.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social features. The only external network communication is with the public MLB data APIs described in Section 4.

9. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. All app data (statistics, preferences) is stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time by deleting the app.

Questions or concerns?
